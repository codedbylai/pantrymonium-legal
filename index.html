Privacy Notice

PantryMonium is designed with privacy as a default. All data you enter — pantry items, receipts, spice rack contents, meal plans, and shopping lists — is stored locally on your device only.

PantryMonium does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. No account is required to use the app. No analytics or tracking SDKs are included in this version.

Future versions may offer optional cloud sync across devices. This will always be opt-in, clearly explained, and subject to an updated privacy notice before activation.

Terms of Use

PantryMonium is provided as-is during early access. The app is intended for personal household food management use only.

Expiry date estimates are based on standard food safety guidelines from Food Standards Australia New Zealand (FSANZ) and should be used as a guide only. Always use your own judgement when assessing whether food is safe to consume.

The developer accepts no liability for food safety decisions made using this app.

PantryMonium is free to use. Optional donations support ongoing development and are entirely voluntary.
